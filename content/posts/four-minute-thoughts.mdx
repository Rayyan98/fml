There's this idea that four minutes is all it takes to change your mind about something. Not in the grand, life-altering sense — though sometimes that too — but in the quiet way. The length of a song. The time it takes to read something that sticks.

That's what this place is for. Short pieces about whatever I'm thinking about. Could be something I built, something I read, a place I went, or just a thought that wouldn't leave me alone.

## Why write at all?

I've started blogs before. They usually die after the second post, suffocated by self-imposed standards and an imaginary audience. This time the deal is different: if it takes more than four minutes to read, it's too long. If it takes more than an evening to write, I'm overthinking it.

## What to expect

A mix of things:

- **Tech stuff** — things I learn, things I break, things I build
- **Life stuff** — observations, recommendations, small reviews
- **Ideas** — half-formed thoughts I want to put somewhere outside my head

No schedule. No promises. Just a place that exists.

Welcome.
